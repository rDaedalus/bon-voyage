ORIGIN OF THE LOGO

The term 'Bon Voyage' is a French expression that translates to express good wishes to someone about to go on a journey. As a company who specializes in making people feel at ease when booking their flights, our services will ensure them to have a safe flight without experiencing hassle, and making them feel safe. The airplane that can be seen in the logo is used to represent the airline. Lastly, the image that shows Philippine islands represents the origin of the venture, and the Filipino people who are behind it and will benefit from it.​

BACKGROUND OF THE COMPANY

Bon Voyage Airlines (BVA) is an online platform that was made in 2021 to respond to the changing preferences of travelers from local and international schemes. BVA is a recently launched as an online travel agency (OTA) that is based in the Philippines where people can book their ticket through various locations, both domestic and international flights. Our duty is to provide our consumers with the lowest fare in their dream destination and then book directly with without hassle in their chosen airlines.​
